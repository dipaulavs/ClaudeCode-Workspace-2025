## Core Four Methods (Detalhado)

### 1. Warm Outreach (`section_004_002.md`)

**Definição:** Contato privado 1-1 com pessoas que já te conhecem.

**Processo ACA:**
- **Acknowledge:** Reconhecer relacionamento
- **Ask:** Perguntar se conhecem alguém que precise
- **Ask Again:** Pedir apresentação

**Benchmarks:**
- 10% engagement rate (mínimo aceitável)
- 20%+ engagement rate (excelente)

**Custos:** Praticamente zero (só tempo)

---

### 2. Post Free Content (`section_004_003.md` + `section_004_004.md`)

#### Part I: Hook-Retain-Reward

**Content Unit = Hook + Retain + Reward**

##### Hook (3 componentes)

**A) Topics (5 tipos):**
1. **Far Past:** Lições importantes da vida (ex: "I complained I didn't have time...")
2. **Recent Past:** Conversas, meetings, casos de cliente (semana passada)
3. **Present:** Ideias acontecendo AGORA (tweet no momento)
4. **Trending:** O que está viral (apply sua expertise)
5. **Manufactured:** Criar experiência (ex: "lived on $100 for a month")

**B) Headlines (7 componentes):**
Meta-análise de notícias. Incluir pelo menos 2:
1. **Recency:** "An hour ago" > "A year ago"
2. **Relevancy:** Significativo para o público (nurses care about nurse stuff)
3. **Celebrity:** Pessoas proeminentes (Jeff Bezos breakfast)
4. **Proximity:** Geograficamente próximo (neighbor's house on fire)
5. **Conflict:** Ideias/pessoas opostas (Pineapple on pizza?, Good vs Evil)
6. **Unusual:** Raro, bizarro (six-fingered man)
7. **Ongoing:** Histórias em progresso (someone in labor - updates every 10min)

**C) Format:**
- Match plataforma (look like stuff they've liked before)
- Audience expectation (LinkedIn ≠ Instagram ≠ Twitter)

##### Retain (3 formas de curiosidade)

**Curiosity = Unresolved questions** (explicit or implicit)

**A) Lists:**
- Items apresentados um após outro
- Número no headline ("7 Ways...", "28 Ways To Stay Poor")
- Segue tema comum

**B) Steps:**
- Ações em ordem que alcançam objetivo
- Menos flexível que lists, mais explícito reward
- Ex: "3 Steps to Creating a Great Hook"

**C) Stories:**
- Eventos reais ou imaginários
- Lesson/takeaway para listener
- Ex: Quase todo capítulo do livro tem história

**Combinar:** Lists dentro de steps, story sobre cada list item, etc.

##### Reward

**Princípio:** Value per second

- Não existe "too long", só "too boring"
- Pessoa que se entedia em 3s de vídeo pode binge 900-page book
- Clearly satisfy hook promise
- Audience decides se recompensa foi boa (não você)

**Indicador de sucesso:** Audience grows

### 3. Cold Outreach (`section_004_006.md`)

**Definição:** Contato privado 1-1 com strangers.

**3 Problemas + 3 Soluções:**

| Problema | Solução |
|----------|---------|
| Não tem como contatar | Build a List (Software → Brokers → Elbow Grease) |
| Ignoram você | Personalize + Big Fast Value |
| Baixo volume de respostas | Automate + Follow up |

**Personalization:**
- 1-3 info que amigo saberia
- Complement + show how benefited you
- Personalizar subject lines, primeiras mensagens

**Big Fast Value:**
- Não tickle interest, BLOW MINDS em 30s
- Give away stuff people normally PAY for
- Lead magnet 3x melhor = take rates 3x

**Volume:**
- Automated Delivery (pre-recorded voicemail, templated email)
- Automated Distribution (robot dial, blast 1000 emails)
- Follow up: Multiple times, multiple ways, multiple months (3-6 months cycle)

**Benchmarks:**
- Phone: 20% pickup rate, 25% lead magnet take = 4 engaged leads/100 calls
- Email: 30% open, 10% reply = 3% engagement
- DM: 20% reply (personal video/voice memo)
- Target: 3%+ lead engagement rate

**Costs:** Labor intensive ($15/hr + $50/show + leads $0.10)

**7 Benefícios:**
1. No content/ad creation needed
2. Competition won't know (private)
3. Incredibly reliable (input = output)
4. Fewer platform changes
5. Compliance less painful
6. No spokesperson = Sellable business
7. Hard to copy

### 4. Affiliates and Partners (`section_005_005.md`)

**Lead-getters independentes que promovem seu produto**

**6 Steps to Build Affiliate Army:**

1. **Find Ideal Affiliate:**
   - Who's got my leads?
   - 6 categorias: Software, Products, Equipment, Services, Groups, Events

2. **Make Them An Offer:**
   - Callout (spa owners, busy professionals, etc)
   - Value elements (make more money, no delivery/support, start tomorrow)

3. **Qualify Them:**
   - Make them a customer (buy/use product)
   - Make them an expert (certification 10-20% of annual earnings)

4. **Figure Out What To Pay:**
   - What they get paid for (new customers, repeat customers)
   - How much (based on max allowable CAC)
   - 3-tier structure: 25% CAC (anyone), 50% CAC (activate), 100% CAC (sustain)

5. **Get Them Advertising - Launch:**
   - **Whisper-Tease-Shout** method
   - Whisper (curiosity, behind scenes) → 4-6 weeks until 60 days out
   - Tease (elements of value, What-Who-When) → 1x/week until 14 days, 2x/week until 3 days
   - Shout (CTA, bonuses, scarcity, urgency) → 2x/day 3 days out, every 30min launch day

6. **Keep Them Advertising - Integrate:**
   - Strategy 1: Give your lead magnet away (with their core offer)
   - Strategy 2: Sell your lead magnet (they keep 100%)
   - Strategy 3: Sell your core offer (split money)

**Costs/Returns:**
- Compare LTGP of all customers affiliate brings vs CAC to get affiliate
- Target 5:1, 10:1+ ratio
- Example: $4k CAC → $54k LTGP = 12.5:1

**Case Studies:**
- National Tax Preparation ($50M via free LLC setup)
- Prestige Labs (4000 gyms x 14 sales/month)
- Chiropractors (workshops at gyms)

## Fórmulas e Cálculos

### LTGP (Lifetime Gross Profit)
```
LTGP = (Total Revenue) - (Cost of Goods/Fulfillment)
```

### CAC (Cost to Acquire Customer)
```
CAC = (Total Ad Spend) / (Number of Customers)
```

### LTGP:CAC Ratio
```
LTGP:CAC = LTGP / CAC

Mínimo aceitável: 3:1
Bom: 5:1
Excelente: 10:1+
```

### Affiliate Payout
```
Max Allowable CAC = LTGP / 3

3-Tier Structure:
- Tier 1: 25% of Max CAC (anyone who signs up)
- Tier 2: 50% of Max CAC (activate)
- Tier 3: 100% of Max CAC (sustain performance)

Average Payout = (20% x Tier1) + (20% x Tier2) + (60% x Tier3)
```

## Action Steps (Principais do Livro)

### Post Free Content
- Life happens–profit by sharing yours (topics)
- Include 2+ headline components
- Format for platform first, then tweak for audience
- Use lists, steps, stories to embed curiosity
- Provide more value than anyone else

### Cold Outreach
- Find scraping tool/broker, assemble 1000 names
- Do research on each lead (batch work)
- Provide biggest fastest value you can afford
- Contact multiple times in multiple ways
- Wait 3-6 months, then start again

### Affiliates
- Make sheet with questions/categories, fill online
- Explore value elements, fill in blanks (make affiliates the customer)
- Make affiliates customers, experts, or both
- Figure out what to pay for, how much
- Start whispering 4-6 weeks out
- Integrate: choose give away/sell lead magnet/sell core offer
