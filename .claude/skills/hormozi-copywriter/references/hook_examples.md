# Hormozi Hook Examples - 30+ Proven Hooks

## Formula Base

**Structure:** Effort High + Result Low ‚Üí Change Small + Result Massive

**Mandatory Elements (minimum 3 numbers):**
- Specific numbers (time, money, quantity)
- Timeline (days/months/years)
- Contradiction (opposite of expected)
- Social proof / authority failure
- Pain of silence (intimate shame/frustration)
- The turn (don't reveal method)
- Before/after measurable

## Success Patterns Identified

### Structures That Always Work

1. **High Effort + Low Result ‚Üí Minimal Change + High Result**
   - "Did X for Y time. Z bad result. Changed ONE thing. W incredible result."

2. **High Financial Investment + Worsening ‚Üí Cheap/Simple Solution**
   - "Spent R$ X in Y months. Z got worse. Discovered it just took [simple]."

3. **Authority Failed + New Authority Saved**
   - "My [professional X] said Y. Switched. New [professional] did Z."

4. **Repeated Failure + Sudden Turn**
   - "Failed X times in Y time. On attempt Z, changed [detail]. Success."

5. **Common Sense Contradicted + Opposite Result**
   - "Did [what everyone says]. Got worse. Did OPPOSITE. Got better."

### Always Present Elements

- ‚úÖ Concrete numbers (minimum 3 per hook)
- ‚úÖ Specific timeline (days/months/years)
- ‚úÖ Clear contradiction (opposite of expected)
- ‚úÖ Witnesses/social validation

- ‚úÖ Measurable before/after
