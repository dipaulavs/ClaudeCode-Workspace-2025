## Mandatory Elements Checklist

Before finalizing any hook, verify it has:

- [ ] **3+ specific numbers** (money, time, quantity)
- [ ] **Timeline** (days, months, years - not "soon" or "quickly")
- [ ] **Contradiction** (opposite of what's expected)
- [ ] **Social proof OR authority witness** (someone who validated/noticed)
- [ ] **Pain of silence** (intimate frustration, shame, or failure)
- [ ] **The turn** (change point - but don't reveal the method)
- [ ] **Measurable before/after** (quantify the transformation)

## Testing Guidelines

**Create 3-5 variations:**
1. Use different templates
2. Test different numbers
3. Vary the contradiction angle
4. Try different authority figures
5. Adjust timeline emphasis

**Track metrics:**
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Watch time / engagement
- Comments asking "how?"
- Shares / saves

**Double down on winner:**
Once you find a hook pattern that works in your niche, create 10+ variations using the same structure with different specifics.
