# Grand Slam Offer Template (Fill-in-the-Blank)

Based on $100M Offers framework by Alex Hormozi.

---

## Full Offer Structure

```
[Dream Outcome] in [Timeline] or [Guarantee].

[Proof statement with numbers].

[Timeline breakdown with milestones].

[Core offer components].

[Effort required]. [What they DON'T have to do].

[Bonuses stacked].

[Scarcity]. [Urgency].

[Price + Value comparison].
```

---

## Component 1: Dream Outcome + Timeline

**Formula:** [Specific result] in [Exact time] or [Consequence/guarantee]

**Examples:**
```
✅ "Add $50k/month in recurring revenue in 90 days or we work free until you do"
✅ "Lose 20 lbs in 12 weeks and fit into your wedding dress or full refund"
✅ "Get 50 qualified leads/week within 30 days or don't pay"
```

**Fill in:**
```
[_________________] in [_______] or [___________________].
   (Dream Outcome)      (Timeline)     (Guarantee)
```

**Requirements:**
- Dream outcome = specific number (not "grow your business")
- Timeline = exact days/weeks (not "quickly" or "soon")
- Guarantee = clear consequence (refund, work free, etc.)

---

## Component 2: Proof Statement

**Formula:** [X number] clients achieved [specific result] using [unique method]

**Examples:**
```
✅ "147 clients hit $50k/month in 90 days using our Lead Velocity System"
✅ "342 brides lost 20+ lbs in 12 weeks with the Bridal Body Method"
✅ "89 agencies added 10+ clients/month without paid ads using our Cold Outreach Framework"
```

**Fill in:**
```
[_____] clients achieved [__________________] using [____________________].
(Number)                 (Specific result)          (Unique method name)
```

**Requirements:**
- Real number (if small, say "pilot group" or specific time period)
- Specific result (numbers, timeline)
- Named methodology (makes it proprietary)

---

## Component 3: Timeline Breakdown

**Formula:** Show micro-wins at Day 1, Day 7, Day 30, Day 90

**Examples:**
```
✅ "Most clients see:
• Day 3: First inbound lead
• Day 14: First client closed
• Day 90: $50k/month consistent"

✅ "What to expect:
• Week 1: Lose 3-5 lbs (water weight)
• Week 4: Down 8 lbs (visible in mirror)
• Week 12: Hit goal weight (dress fits)"
```

**Fill in:**
```
Most clients see:
• Day [__]: [________________]
          (Early micro-win)
• Day [__]: [________________]
          (Medium milestone)
• Day [__]: [________________]
          (Main goal achieved)
```

**Requirements:**
- Exact days (not "soon" or "later")
- Specific outcomes (quantified)
- Progressive wins (early, middle, final)

---

## Component 4: Core Offer Components

**Formula:** List what they get (done-for-you > done-with-you > do-it-yourself)

**Examples:**
```
✅ "We build your funnels, write all copy, and train your team.
You spend 3 hours/week on sales calls (we book them)."

✅ "Custom meal plans delivered weekly.
20-minute daily workouts (app guided).
Weekly check-ins with your coach."
```

**Fill in:**
```
[Done-for-you component]:
[_________________________________]

[Done-with-you component]:
[_________________________________]

[Templates/tools provided]:
[_________________________________]
```

**Requirements:**
- Lead with highest-effort items (we do the hard stuff)
- Specify time required from them
- Make it sound easier than it is

---

## Component 5: Effort + Sacrifice Addressed

**Formula:** [Time required] [What they DON'T have to do/give up]

**Examples:**
```
✅ "Only 3 hours/week. Keep your current business. No ads budget required."
✅ "20 minutes/day. No gym. No meal prep. Eat pizza once/week."
✅ "15 minutes/morning. No cold calling. No showing up on camera."
```

**Fill in:**
```
[Time]: Only [_____] hours/day or [_____] hours/week

[Don't have to do]: No [_______]. No [_______]. No [_______].

[Don't have to give up]: Keep [_______]. Still [_______].
```

**Requirements:**
- Specific time (not "minimal effort")
- List 3 things they DON'T have to do
- Address what they fear giving up

---

## Component 6: Bonuses (Value Stack)

**Formula:** Bonus [Name] - [Value] + [Why it matters]

**Examples:**
```
✅ "BONUS #1: Done-For-You Funnel Setup ($3,000 value)
We build your entire funnel in first 7 days. You don't touch tech."

✅ "BONUS #2: Pre-Written Email Sequences ($1,500 value)
48 proven emails. Just personalize and send. Avg 22% open rate."

✅ "BONUS #3: 90-Day Implementation Roadmap ($1,000 value)
Know exactly what to do each week. No guessing. Stay on track."
```

**Fill in:**
```
BONUS #1: [________________________] ($[______] value)
          (Bonus name)                   (Value)
[Why it matters / What it does for them]
[_________________________________________]

BONUS #2: [________________________] ($[______] value)
[_________________________________________]

BONUS #3: [________________________] ($[______] value)
[_________________________________________]
```

**Requirements:**
- Each bonus solves ONE specific problem
- Assign dollar value (be reasonable)
- Explain why they need it (not just "nice to have")
- Total bonus value should be 1-2x core price

---

## Component 7: Scarcity

**Formula:** Real limitation (capacity, time, availability)

**Examples:**
```
✅ "Only 10 spots per month (we cap at 10 for quality)"
✅ "Only accepting 5 clients this quarter"
✅ "Only available to first 20 people (software beta)"
```

**Fill in:**
```
Only [_____] [spots/clients/licenses] [per month/this quarter/total].

Reason: [_________________________________]
        (Why is it limited? Capacity, beta, time, etc.)
```

**Requirements:**
- Must be REAL (fake scarcity kills trust)
- Give reason (capacity, quality, beta, etc.)
- Make it verifiable (they can check)

---

## Component 8: Urgency

**Formula:** Deadline + Consequence

**Examples:**
```
✅ "Price increases $500 on [Date]"
✅ "Enrollment closes Friday at midnight"
✅ "Only 3 spots left at this price"
```

**Fill in:**
```
[Action] [happens/closes/increases] on [Date/Countdown].

Consequence: [_________________________________]
            (What they lose if they wait)
```

**Requirements:**
- Specific deadline (date or countdown)
- Clear consequence (price increase, spots gone, etc.)
- Must be REAL (never lie about urgency)

---

## Component 9: Price + Value Stack

**Formula:** Show total value, reveal actual price, calculate savings

**Examples:**
```
✅ "Total Value: $10,500
Your Investment Today: $5,000
You Save: $5,500 (52% off)"

✅ "Core Program: $997
Bonus #1: $3,000
Bonus #2: $1,500
Bonus #3: $1,000
Total Value: $6,497
Today: $997 (Save $5,500)"
```

**Fill in:**
```
Core Offer: $[_______]
Bonus #1: $[_______]
Bonus #2: $[_______]
Bonus #3: $[_______]

Total Value: $[_______]
Your Investment Today: $[_______]
You Save: $[_______] ([__]% off)
```

**Requirements:**
- List each component with value
- Show total value (sum of all)
- Show actual price (should be 1/2 to 1/3 of total value)
- Calculate savings (total - price)

## Checklist: Before Publishing Offer

Verify your offer has:
- [ ] Specific dream outcome (numbers + timeline)
- [ ] Clear guarantee (consequence if fails)
- [ ] Proof statement (X clients, specific results)
- [ ] Timeline breakdown (Day 1, Day 7, Day 30, Day 90)
- [ ] Core components listed (what they get)
- [ ] Effort minimized (time required clearly stated)
- [ ] Sacrifice addressed (what they DON'T have to do)
- [ ] 3+ bonuses (with dollar values + benefits)
- [ ] Real scarcity (capacity/time limitation)
- [ ] Real urgency (deadline + consequence)
- [ ] Value stack (total value vs price)
- [ ] Price anchored (showing savings)
- [ ] Zero vague language ("soon", "quickly", "results may vary")

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Mistake 1: Vague Dream Outcome
"Help you grow your business"
**Fix:** "Add $50k/month in recurring revenue"

### ❌ Mistake 2: No Timeline
"You'll see results"
**Fix:** "Day 14: First client closed"

### ❌ Mistake 3: Weak Guarantee
"Satisfaction guaranteed"
**Fix:** "Hit $50k/month in 120 days or we work free until you do"

### ❌ Mistake 4: Irrelevant Bonuses
"Get my unrelated course as bonus"
**Fix:** Bonuses solve problems on path to dream outcome

### ❌ Mistake 5: Fake Scarcity
"Only 1000 spots!" (unlimited digital product)
**Fix:** Real limitation (capacity, cohort size, beta access)

### ❌ Mistake 6: No Effort Clarity
"It's easy!" (but requires 40 hours/week)
**Fix:** "3 hours/week. We handle the rest."
